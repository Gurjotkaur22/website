About Us

Bia Blooms is a family-owned and operated florist located in Los Angeles, CA. We have been in business for over 20 years, and we take pride in providing our customers with beautiful, high-quality flowers and exceptional customer service.

Our Services

Flower Arrangements

Our talented florists can create stunning floral arrangements for any occasion, including weddings, birthdays, and anniversaries.

Bouquets

We offer a wide variety of beautiful and unique bouquets for any occasion, from romantic gestures to sympathy arrangements.

Plants

Our selection of plants includes everything from small succulents to large potted trees. We can help you find the perfect plant for your home or office.
